Usage Tips

  • Mention You can mention other users in your messages to get them notified. To do that prefix your message with @ followed by the user's ID. For example: @user-12345 How are you? You can mention more than one user in a message. To achieve that, You follow this format: @[user-12345,user-23345] Hello guys!
  • Send private/hidden messages: You can send other users private/hidden messages that others on the chat room won't see except you and the recipient of the message. To do that prefix your message with dot/period (.) followed by the user's ID. For example: .user-12345 How are you? You can send to more than one user in a message. To achieve that, You follow this format: .[user-12345,user-23345] Hello guys!
  • Bot Account (ac) There's a bot account with the name ac that is present in the Chat room. You can mention the bot in your message or send a private message to it and it will respond with random riddles, jokes and facts. The bot also notifies the chat room when people leave or join the chat. For example: @ac hi or .ac hi
  • Leave Chat: To quit the chat, send .q
    While to end the chat immediately without showing the action confirmation dialog, send .q!

Chat Rules

  • Respect Others: Treat all chat participants with respect and kindness. Avoid offensive language, harassment, or bullying of any kind.
  • Stay Anonymous: Refrain from sharing personal information, such as real names, addresses, phone numbers, or social media accounts.
  • No Hate Speech: Do not engage in hate speech, discrimination, or spreading harmful content. Respect the diversity and inclusivity of the chat community.
  • Keep it Safe: Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior to the moderators/administrators immediately.
  • No Spamming: Avoid excessive posting of the same message or irrelevant content. Keep the chat meaningful and engaging.
  • Keep it Fun: Engage in enjoyable and constructive conversations. Remember, the goal is to have a pleasant and interactive chat experience.
